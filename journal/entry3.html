2024/06/29: A Simple Model for Online Message Discipline

Same vibe as using Taylor Swift ticket sales to predict inflation.... read with caution.

The idea that I'm concerned with is this: leftists should speak and act in a fashion that is less off putting and less provocative, in the hopes of building an effective coalition.

Suppose that this is the situation faced by our typical postmodern neo-Marxist serpent (now it turns out that our results depends significantly on this, but expansion+justification later): they come across a discourse where they are faced with the decision of speaking their political minds, or moderating their speech.

Assumption 1: In general, our protagonist prefers speaking their minds to not speaking their minds.

Assumption 2: For simplification (though nothing depends upon this simplification), our protagonist would rather the left win (more supporters for a bill, popular opinion, what not). But if moderating their speech would change a result from losing to winning, they would rather moderate their speech.

Hence, we have a decision model as follows:

Game Matrix: Online Message Discipline
Left Win Left Lose
Speaking Mind 4 2
Message Discipline 3 1

A simple dominance argument would suffice to show that it is always better to speak your minds.
