Tip: Have a really hot wok! And stir fry everything on medium high-high. This might not be a healthy suggestion but....be generous with oil.

Ingredients

  • Hand-pulled noodles (but you can replace this with any other chewy noodles)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • minced garlic
  • Red pepper, sliced
  • 500g beef strips
  • Chinese chives
  • Handful of dried chili
  • Soy sauce
  • Dark Soy sauce
  • Cumin powder
  • Ketchup
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chilli Bean Sauce Ë±ÜÁì£ÈÜ¨ (can be found at chinese grocery stores)
  • MSG (oh yes ü§åüèº)

Instructions

  1. Soak the dried chillis in water for 10-15 minutes, and cut the onion and pepper into strips.
  2. Marinade the beef with black pepper, white pepper, one spoon of soy sauce, one spoon of dark soy soysauce, and cornstarch. Mix well, then rizzle with olive oil and leave in fridge to marinade for 15 minutes.
  3. Boil the noodles for 5 minutes (or however long it takes for it to cook), then wash in cold water to stop the cooking.
  4. Heat up some oil in a HOT (!!) wok, stir-fry the beef until browned, then set aside.
  5. Add the sliced garlic and the white parts of chinese chives, and cook until fragrant. Add a spoon of chilli bean sauce, dried chillies, onions, and pepper.
  6. Add one spoon of the following: ketchup, soysauce, dark soysauce, oyster sauce, and a pinch of white pepper and MSG. Add cumin to taste.
  7. Add the beef, the noodles, and chinese chives.
  8. Top with sesame seeds.
